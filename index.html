Proof-first credibility

Autokirk is designed as an AI governance control plane. The Operator Console is the user-facing surface that earns trust through verifiable outcomes.

Governed execution

Every meaningful action is gated by explicit human authorization. No approval, no execution.

Single-use approvals

Replay attempts are blocked. Approvals are consumed atomically on success.

Auditable proof trail

Before/after state, rationale, and timestamps are written as proof artifacts for inspection and reporting.

The operator loop

Simple, deterministic, and built to scale with governance—then velocity.
