# Face #003 Launch Checklist (Relief-First MEL)

## Goal
Prove daily closure behavior before automation.

## Week 1 Rules
- Manual lead entry allowed
- Manual sale closure entry allowed
- No CRM/DMS integrations
- No dashboards

## MEL Definition
Lead -> Obligation -> Deadline -> Closure -> Ledger

## Success Conditions
- 10+ obligations created by Day 3
- 5+ closures recorded by Day 3
- 50 closures recorded before expansion

## Expansion Gate
No new screens/features until:
- ≥50 closures AND
- ≥1 escalation handled
